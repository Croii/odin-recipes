Frozen peanut butter cups

Peanut butter cups containing and dark chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoons butter
  • 5 tablespoons dark chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 6 digestive biscuits
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Grease a 12-hole cupcake tray (or use a 24-hole mini cupcake tray).
  2. Melt the chocolate in short bursts on high in a microwave, stirring often.
  3. (You can also melt it in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of barely simmering water, making sure the base of the bowl doesnâ€™t touch the water.)
  4. Carefully spoon over the melted dark chocolate, then pop the tray into the freezer for two to three hours, until frozen solid. Pop out the cups from the moulds and enjoy, or store in a resealable bag in the freezer.
